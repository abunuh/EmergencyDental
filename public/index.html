What We Treat

Emergency & General Dental Services

From sudden toothaches to routine care, we provide comprehensive dental services at prices you can afford.

Emergency Toothache

Immediate pain relief for severe toothaches caused by infection, decay, or nerve damage.

Broken & Chipped Teeth

Fast repair of cracked, broken, or chipped teeth to restore your smile and prevent further damage.

Dental Abscess / Infection

Urgent treatment for painful dental abscesses and infections before they become serious health risks.

Lost Fillings & Crowns

Quick re-cementing or replacement of lost fillings and crowns to protect exposed teeth.

Extractions

Gentle tooth extractions including wisdom teeth — performed with care to minimize discomfort.

Cleanings & Exams

Routine cleanings, X-rays, and exams scheduled around your busy life — including weekends.
